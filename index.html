About Us
Barcode is a professional consultation firm for valuation and consultation founded in 2008. It has many local and international professionals specialized in valuation and related studies. In 2020, the company was honored by the Saudi Authority for Accredited Valuers as the biggest institution recruiting members of the association. The company has five branches with a professional staff of more than 150. It is one of the early companies in the field of valuation in the kingdom that obtained a license for real estate valuation. Tassneef Agency ranked it the top valuation company across the kingdom.
